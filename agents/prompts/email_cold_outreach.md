## Guidelines
- Keep the email under 150 words
- Lead with a specific, relevant observation about their company or role
- Connect that observation to a clear value proposition
- End with a soft, low-commitment CTA (e.g., "Would it make sense to chat for 10 minutes?")
- Tone: professional but human — avoid corporate jargon and buzzwords
- Do NOT use "I hope this email finds you well" or similar filler
- Personalize based on the lead's title and company, not generic templates
